How to Use the Snowbound LLC Calendar Site

Go to Login Page

This guide walks you through everything you can do on the new Snowbound LLC condo calendar website. The new site replaces the old Google Sheets calendar.

What changed and why:

  • The old calendar on Google Sheets was open to anyone with the link — no login was required. That meant the lock box code, garage code, and WiFi password were visible to the entire internet.
  • The new site requires you to log in with your email address. No password to remember — you just click a link in your email.
  • Everything else works the same way it always has. Same calendar, same trading, same owner information.

1. Logging In (Magic Link)

The new site uses a "magic link" to log you in. There is no password to create or remember. Here's how it works:

1 Go to 69hawn.com and click "Click Here For Secure Site to view / modify the calendar."
2 You will see a login page. Type in your email address — the same one you use for Snowbound communications — and click "Send Magic Link."
3 Check your email. You will receive a message from 69hawn@gmail.com with a subject line of "Snowbound LLC Calendar — Login Link." Click the link in the email.
4 That's it — you're logged in! The site will open to the calendar. You'll stay logged in for 7 days before needing to log in again.
Tip: The magic link expires after 15 minutes and can only be used once. If it expires, just go back and request a new one.
Important: You must use the email address that is on file with Snowbound LLC. If you're not sure which email to use, ask Larry.

2. Viewing the Calendar

After you log in, the calendar is the first thing you see. It shows who has the condo each week for the entire year.

What you'll see:

  • Top rows: The garage code, WiFi password, and lock box code, along with neighbor contact information.
  • Left column: Each owner's name, email, and phone number.
  • Week columns (1 through 5): The dates for each of that owner's weeks. If a week has been traded, you'll see who it was traded to (shown in red).

To look at a different year:

  • Click the left arrow (← 2025) to go to the previous year.
  • Click the right arrow (2027 →) to go to the next year.
  • Use the dropdown in the middle to jump to any year.
Tip: You can print the calendar by clicking the "Print" button in the top right area of the calendar page.

3. Looking Up Your Weeks

The Lookup page lets you quickly find when your weeks are (or look up any other owner's weeks).

1 Click "Lookup" in the menu bar at the top of the page.
2 Choose how to search:
  • "Original Owner" — shows all weeks originally assigned to that owner, even if they traded some away.
  • "Current Holder" — shows only weeks that owner actually has right now, including any they received in a trade.
3 Select your name (or "All Owners") from the owner list.

4. Trading, Giving Away, or Not Using a Week

The Trade/Comment page is where you manage your weeks. You must be logged in to use it.

1 Click "Trade/Comment" in the menu bar.
2 Choose a Trade Type from the dropdown:
Type What it does
Trade Week Swap your week with another owner's week. You pick both owners and both weeks.
Give Away Let everyone know your week is available if someone wants it.
Not Using Let everyone know you will not be using your week.
Using Condo Confirm that you will be using your week.
Comment Add a note to a week without changing anything else.
3 Select Owner 1 (usually yourself) and then select the week from the dropdown. The week list loads automatically after you pick an owner.
4 If you chose "Trade Week," a second set of fields will appear. Select Owner 2 and their week.
5 Add a comment if you'd like (optional, up to 40 characters).
6 Click "Submit."
Tip: After you submit, an email notification is automatically sent to the owners involved in the trade.

5. Adding Weeks to Your Phone or Computer Calendar

You can download a calendar file that adds your condo weeks to Google Calendar, iPhone Calendar, Outlook, or any other calendar app.

1 Click "Download .ics" in the menu bar.
2 Select your name from the owner list.
3 Select the year.
4 Click "Download .ics File."

What happens next depends on your device:

  • On an iPhone or iPad: Tap the downloaded file. It will ask if you want to add the events to your calendar. Tap "Add All."
  • On Android: Tap the downloaded file. It will open in Google Calendar and ask you to add the events.
  • On a computer: Double-click the downloaded file. It will open in your default calendar app (Outlook, Apple Calendar, etc.).
Tip: The downloaded file includes all of your weeks for the selected year, including any trades. Each event shows who currently has that week.

6. Emailing All Owners

Need to send a message to all owners? The Email page sets everything up for you.

1 Click "Email" in the menu bar.
2 Click one of the buttons:
  • "Gmail (web)" — opens a new Gmail message with everyone in BCC.
  • "Outlook (web)" — opens a new Outlook message with everyone in BCC.
  • "Default Mail App" — opens whatever email app is on your phone or computer.
  • "Copy All Addresses" — copies all the email addresses so you can paste them yourself.
Tip: BCC is checked by default, which means the recipients won't see each other's email addresses. This is the recommended setting.

8. Viewing the Activity Log

The Activity page shows a record of every trade, comment, and change that has been made to the calendar. This is useful if you want to see what's been happening or verify a trade went through.

1 Click "Activity" in the menu bar.
2 Use the Year dropdown to pick which year you want to see. It defaults to the current year.

Each row shows when the change was made, who made it, what type of change it was, which owners and weeks were involved, and any comments.

Tip: You can click on the column headers to sort the results.

10. Troubleshooting

"I never received the magic link email."

  • Check your Spam or Junk folder.
  • Make sure you typed the email address that is on file with Snowbound LLC. If you're not sure, ask Larry.
  • The email comes from 69hawn@gmail.com with a subject line of "Snowbound LLC Calendar — Login Link." If you still don't see it after a minute or two, try again.

"The magic link says it's expired."

  • Magic links are valid for 15 minutes. Go back to the login page and request a new one.

"I can't see Trade/Comment or Email in the menu."

  • These only appear when you are logged in. Click "Login" first.

"The calendar looks too small on my phone."

  • Try turning your phone sideways (landscape mode). The calendar is a wide table and is easier to read in landscape.
  • You can also pinch to zoom on any phone or tablet.

"I made a mistake on a trade. How do I undo it?"

  • Submit another trade to reverse it, or contact Larry for help.

"Who do I contact if something isn't working?"

  • Contact Larry Kamons. His information is in the Owner Directory.
